Arielle Tatum

Background

Arielle Tatum is a second-year student at the University of Pretoria, pursuing her BCom Informatics degree. Originally from the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC), Arielle brings a unique perspective to her studies. She is passionate about technology and how it can be applied to solve business challenges.

Throughout her academic journey, Arielle has developed a strong interest in the integration of business and information systems, believing that technology plays a critical role in modernizing industries.

Her cultural background and diverse experiences have shaped her approach to problem-solving, and she is determined to use her knowledge to drive innovation in the field of business informatics.

Hobbies

  • Basketball: Arielle has a deep love for basketball and enjoys both playing and watching the sport. She is passionate about teamwork, strategy, and staying fit through the game.
  • Athletics: Alongside basketball, Arielle is also an avid track and field enthusiast, particularly enjoying sprints and long-distance running. Athletics helps her stay disciplined and competitive.
  • Korean Movies: Arielle has a strong passion for Korean films and dramas. She enjoys exploring Korean culture through its cinematic art and is always on the lookout for new releases in the genre.
  • Traveling: With a love for exploring new places, Arielle enjoys traveling and experiencing different cultures, especially when it comes to experiencing the diversity of African and Asian cultures.
